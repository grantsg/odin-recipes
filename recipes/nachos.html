Cheese Nachos

Description

Grab a nice cold tecate and throw on some shredded cheese on a pile of chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of tortilla chips
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese

Directions

  1. Put chips on a microwave safe plate.
  2. Add cheese on top of the chips.
  3. Microwave on high for 1 to 1 1/2 minutes.
