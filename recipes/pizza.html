Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Not much better comfort food than cheese, bread and some salty meats.

Fire up the oven and throw a couple ingredients together.

Ingredient

  • Premade pizza crust packet
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk mozzarella cheese shredded
  • 9-10 pepperoni slices

Directions

  1. Preheat oven as hot as it'll go. 500 or 550 should suffice
  2. Lay out pizza crust on a pizza pan
  3. Spread out pizza sauce onto crust
  4. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top of the pizza sauce
  5. Lay out pepperoni slices evenly throughout the surface
  6. Bake for 8-12 minutes until cheese is bubbly and brown and crust is desired crispiness
